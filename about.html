Perseverance - 1. To continue a course of action in spite of difficulty, opposition, or discouragement. 2. Remain steadfast

This one word has guided me my entire life. My parents taught me it by giving me a poster with this exact definition alongside Cal Ripken Jr, my favorite childhood athlete.

Passion and loyalty are the two other values that I hold most deeply.

Given this, it's perhaps not too surprising that I identify as an entrepreneur as well as a distance runner. These two passions continue to challenge me every single day of my life and I love it.

I also enjoy many other things: reading, cooking, traveling, and wine. I love to learn new things. Most recently I've taken up Mandarin Chinese.

Family is the most important part of my life -- which, of course, includes my wonderful girlfriend.
